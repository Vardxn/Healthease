# HEALTHEASE Application Screenshots

This document lists the core application views and features, serving as a gallery guide for the portfolio layout.

---

## 📸 Portfolio Gallery

### 1. Patient Dashboard
*The central command page showing the patient's hero greeting, quick actions, upcoming consultations, medication schedule list, and the new **Smart Health Score** Hero Card.*
![Dashboard](./screenshots/dashboard.png)

### 2. Smart Health Score Analytics
*The dedicated health score page featuring detailed analytics, factor progress bars (Medication Adherence, Consultation Attendance, BP, Blood Sugar, Weight, and Logging Consistency), historical progress charts, and medical recommendations.*
![Health Score Page](./screenshots/health_score.png)

### 3. OCR Prescription Reader
*The automated digitization pipeline. Users drag and drop doctor prescriptions to trigger the AI parser which extracts medicine names, frequencies, dosages, and durations instantly.*
![Prescription Reader](./screenshots/ocr_upload.png)

### 4. Vitals Dashboard & Trends
*Line charts charting Blood Pressure, Blood Sugar, Oxygen levels, and body weight over 30 days, alongside the newly integrated **Health Score Trend** chart.*
![Vitals Dashboard](./screenshots/vitals_dashboard.png)

### 5. Medication Tracker & Reminders
*Manage active medications, log daily doses, check stock levels, and review today's schedule with checkoff alerts.*
![Medication Tracker](./screenshots/medicine_tracker.png)

### 6. Telemedicine Consultation Marketplace
*Filter and connect with registered doctors across multiple specializations, view consultation histories, and open completed doctor summaries.*
![Doctor Directory](./screenshots/doctor_directory.png)

### 7. AI Health Assistant
*An interactive chatbot interface that welcomes patients with their current Health Score and answers inquiries regarding drug interactions, history summaries, or vitals stability.*
![AI Assistant](./screenshots/ai_assistant.png)

### 8. Admin Control Center
*Control panel for platform administrators to review doctor applications, approve system credentials, and audit telemetry metrics.*
![Admin Dashboard](./screenshots/admin_dashboard.png)

### 9. Dark Mode Showcase
*Sleek visual hierarchy and high-contrast color scheme rendering perfectly across all components, widgets, and charts.*
