# Healthease Screenshot Guide

To complete your portfolio presentation, take high-quality screenshots of the running application and save them to this directory (`docs/screenshots/`).

## 📋 Naming Convention Checklist

Save your screenshots exactly as named below to ensure the markdown references load correctly:

1. **`landing-page.png`**
   - The public landing page explaining features and pricing.
2. **`dashboard.png`**
   - Main patient dashboard showcasing the hero greeting and **Smart Health Score** Hero Card.
3. **`doctor-directory.png`**
   - Specialist listings marketplace where patients find and book appointments.
4. **`upload-prescription.png`**
   - OCR page showing upload areas and confirmed medication tables.
5. **`medicine-tracker.png`**
   - Daily drug trackers, compliance ratios, and adherence points.
6. **`vitals-dashboard.png`**
   - 30-day vital charts showing BP, Blood Sugar, SpO2, and weight history.
7. **`health-score.png`**
   - Dedicated analytics page displaying factor breakdowns and health suggestions.
8. **`notifications.png`**
   - Live notification center tray and past alert listings.
9. **`admin-dashboard.png`**
   - Admin control panel verifying medical practitioners.
10. **`assistant.png`**
    - The AI chatbot conversation window demonstrating contextual feedback.

## 💡 Pro Tips for Screenshots
- **Resolution**: Use a standard 16:9 ratio screen, preferably 1920x1080 resolution.
- **Theme**: Take screenshots in both light and dark mode, or pick your most polished system theme (dark mode is recommended for SaaS appeal).
- **Data Presence**: Ensure the page has active, populated records (e.g. log a couple of vitals first so the charts render nicely).
