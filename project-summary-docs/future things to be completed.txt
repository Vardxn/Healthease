# HealthEase — Feature Expansion Roadmap (Merged Addition)

Here's a comprehensive breakdown of all features that can be added, prioritized by impact and feasibility.

---

## 🔴 HIGH PRIORITY (Core Healthcare Value)

**1. Appointment & Doctor Management**
- Book appointments with doctors (calendar integration)
- Doctor profile pages with specialization, ratings, availability
- Video consultation via WebRTC or Twilio
- Appointment reminders via SMS/email/push notifications

**2. Medicine Tracker & Smart Reminders**
- Daily pill tracker with check-off system
- Missed dose alerts via push notifications
- Refill reminders based on dosage duration
- Drug interaction checker (OpenFDA API)

**3. Health Vitals Dashboard**
- Log vitals: BP, sugar, weight, SpO2, heart rate
- Historical graphs with trend analysis (Chart.js / Recharts)
- Abnormality detection with alerts (e.g., BP > 140)
- Export vitals as PDF report for doctor visits

**4. Emergency SOS & Medical ID**
- One-tap SOS button → calls emergency contact + shares live location
- Offline Medical ID card (blood group, allergies, conditions, emergency contacts)
- QR code on profile for quick scan by paramedics

**5. Family Health Management**
- Add family members under one account (parent/child/spouse)
- Separate health profiles per member
- Unified family dashboard for caregivers

---

## 🟡 MEDIUM PRIORITY (Engagement & Depth)

**6. Lab Report Digitization**
- Upload blood test / lab reports (PDF or image)
- OCR + AI parsing of test values (Hemoglobin, HbA1c, etc.)
- Flag out-of-range values with reference ranges
- Track lab values over time with graphs

**7. Symptom Checker**
- Input symptoms → AI suggests probable conditions
- Urgency rating: Self-care / See a doctor / Emergency
- Powered by a medical LLM prompt or Infermedica API

**8. Health Insurance Wallet**
- Store insurance card details and policy documents
- Claim tracking and history
- Hospital network search by insurer

**9. Diet & Nutrition Tracker**
- Log meals with calorie/macro info (Nutritionix API)
- Condition-specific diet plans (diabetic, hypertensive, etc.)
- Integration with prescriptions to flag food-drug interactions

**10. Mental Health Module**
- Daily mood tracker (emoji-based)
- Guided breathing exercises and meditation timers
- Stress/anxiety self-assessment (PHQ-9, GAD-7 scales)
- Anonymous journaling with AI sentiment feedback

**11. Multilingual & Accessibility Support**
- UI in Hindi, Urdu, Bengali, Tamil, etc. (i18next)
- Voice interface in regional languages (Bhashini/Sarvam already integrated)
- Screen reader compatibility (ARIA labels)
- High-contrast and font-size settings

**12. Health News & Education Feed**
- Curated health articles by user's conditions/profile
- Medication info cards (side effects, usage tips)
- Weekly health tips from AI based on user data

---

## 🟢 LOW PRIORITY (Premium / Future Growth)

**13. Doctor/Clinician Portal**
- Separate doctor login with patient management dashboard
- Doctors can verify/update prescriptions digitally
- Patient history visible to assigned doctors only
- E-prescription generation and digital signature

**14. Wearable & IoT Integration**
- Sync with Apple Health / Google Fit / Fitbit
- Auto-import step count, sleep data, heart rate
- Smartwatch alerts for medicine reminders

**15. Telemedicine Marketplace**
- Browse and book verified doctors by specialty
- Secure in-app messaging with doctors
- Payment gateway (Razorpay / Stripe) for consultations
- Prescription issuance post-consultation

**16. Gamification & Health Score**
- Personal "Health Score" (0–100) based on vitals, medication adherence, activity
- Streak rewards for logging vitals consistently
- Badges and milestones for health goals

**17. Audit Logs & Compliance**
- Full activity log (who accessed what, when)
- HIPAA/DPDP Act compliance hardening
- Data export (right to portability)
- Account deletion with data purge

**18. Admin & Analytics Panel**
- Platform usage metrics
- User growth, prescription uploads, chatbot usage
- Flagged/abnormal health reports review
- Moderation tools for doctor verification
